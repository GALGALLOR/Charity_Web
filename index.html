Giving is Virtue.

Street Relief Care is a community initiative founded in February 2024 in Chicago, Illinois. Its mission is to provide comprehensive support to homeless and food-insecure individuals through food aid, to address the critical challenges faced by those living without food in the city. The organization aims to uplift individuals and foster sustainable community growth by not only providing food and nutrition.

Our Gallery

Our gallery is a collection of activities that accelerate our goal to significantly reduce hunger and thirst among homeless populations by establishing sustainable food and water distribution networks. We will also implement educational initiatives on nutrition and hydration and facilitate referrals and support services to break the cycle of homelessness.

How we started.

We started off by helping the homeless communities in the Northern Chicago area in February of 2024 and we have been driving the organization forcefully ever since. We aim to put a smile of everyone's face because not everyone chooses to live in a home, it is usually circumstancial.

Leadership

STREET RELIEF CARE is a minority and migrant, women & youth-focused organisation, governed by a Director and Program coordinator. The Director is responsible for policy formation, fund-raising, lobbying and advocacy, financial oversight, and overall decision-making, including oversight over the Management Team. The day-to-day affairs of the organization are managed by a 3-member Management Team supported by a competent and dedicated Programme Team and a group of volunteers. STREET RELIEF CARE has a coordination office in Chicago, Illinois.

Mussa Ali

Exceutive Director

STREET RELIEF CARE has developed clear organization policies and guidelines that stipulate the roles and responsibilities of different institutions and the duties and responsibilities of all staff that guide their operations. We seek consensus in our endeavors and strive to build a transparent and accountable organization that serves the interests of homeless people. We comply with all laid-out regulations of a functional communal organization.
